This year the Centre for Information Discovery and Data Analytics (CIDDA) at RMIT is hosting CReD'21 (CIDDA Research Day) as a face-to-face event.  

You are invited to participate in the CIDDA Research Day (CReD’21) to be held on Friday, 3rd December 2021. We are hoping that this event provides an opportunity for our research students to present their work to CIDDA academic staff and to their peers while having fun!  
 
We plan to have poster presentations, a panel session, and a networking session along with other fun activities.  
 
As a CIDDA student, Postdoc, academic member you are encouraged to participate by providing a poster to display your research.  
 
We are still finalising the details, but we hope to have some prizes for the best poster. Please note only students are eligible for prizes. 
 
The poster can be a new or an academic poster that you have already produced for a conference or other showcase. There is no specific template for posters. However, please take the following guidelines into consideration or your submission may not be accepted: 

The poster must showcase ongoing research that has been accepted or presented recently or the topics of interest/expertise you would like to share with others. 
Include author(s) name(s) and author(s) affiliation(s). 
The size of the poster is required to be in a standard A0 portrait format. The A0 paper size is 84.1 by 118.9 cm (33.1 in × 46.8 in). 


The only accepted file format is PDF. Use high quality converter and make sure you set the paper size as specified above. 
Submissions will be accepted until Friday 26 November 2021, 11:59 pm. Early submission is highly encouraged.  

Please submit your files to here with the following information: Name of the presenter, the group (I.e., CIDDA’s research theme) and the topic of the research (please use one topic). 
Link:https: Registration Form 
 
Please note that at least one of the authors should attend the poster session with their printed posters and present the work to be eligible for the awards. 
P.S. If you are supervising students or postdocs, please feel free to forward this invitation to them. 
 
If you have any enquiry, please feel free to contact Sarah Almaghrabi via: ​sarah.almaghrabi@student.rmit.edu.au. 
 
We hope to see you at CReD’21!
