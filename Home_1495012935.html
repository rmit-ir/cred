​CIDDA Research Day (CReD)

​​CReD is a student led, annual event that showcases the research potential of CIDDA research students and academic staff. The first CReD was held in 2019 with the aim of creating a space to get to know the talent within the CIDDA community and to foster potential collaboration among our unique and interdisciplinary team.
